This is a custom style that provides a compact view for outlook office365. The style will reduce whitespace, margins, borders, icons to maximize your workspace.


Currently enabled on :

  • http://outlook.office.com
  • https://outlook.office.com
  • http://outlook.office365.com
  • https://outlook.office365.com


Limited adjustments on Hotmail/Live.com:

  • http://outlook.live.com
  • https://outlook.live.com


Why use Compact Views:

  • Improve overall use of workspace by limiting pagination, and scrolling.
  • This really comes down to readability and what makes the most sense when glancing at the email screen
  • Windows Desktop Outlook has an Inbox feature called Compact Layout why not Office365?
